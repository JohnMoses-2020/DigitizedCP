Who We Are

All-In-One Team.



Digitized CP is a full-service digital marketing company committed to the development and improvement of your online presence. We take pride in establishing and maintaining professional digital identities for businesses and corporations who seek our help. Our team is composed of skilled university students specializing in fields such as business administration, marketing psychology, graphic design, web design, photography, cinematography, and digital marketing.

Our Work

Digitized CP focuses on the management and digital marketing of your social media pages. We create digital advertisements such as graphic designs, digital videos, and personalized posts. Furthermore, we implement the C.A.T. System in the creation of posts on your social media page. We:

  • Create advertisement messages for posts of your social media page and incorporate concepts of marketing psychology and behavioral economics with the help and supervision of our certified marketing psychologist.
  • Analyze the quantitative statistics of each post (such as the number of clicks, actions, and engagement of each post, as well as the age and sex demographic) to determine what is your target audience and which type of message generates the most traction for your business.
  • Test the messages that had the most traction to generate the most amount of interest within your social media page and maximize the number of potential clients.

Our Mission

We are determined to give you the tools necessary to develop and cement the presence of your business in the digital marketplace. Furthermore, we will develop, establish, and maintain a loyal, dedicated community in your social media platform to help you achieve success in the digital market. Additionally, we are eager to collaborate with other independent companies in which we can mutually work together to promote the growth of these local and independent businesses that provide excellent services and have the potential to grow beyond their current level.

Vision & Purpose

As young entrepreneurs who have been exposed to technology and social media from a very early age, we understand the potential that social media marketing has for both independent, local, and large businesses. Furthermore, we know that it can be difficult to manage a social media page in an efficient and effective way that will yield the most results. We strongly believe that local and even larger businesses have the potential to grow far beyond what they believe they can. Our purpose is to:

  • Create a loyal and stable community by engaging with your followers and clients within your social media platform.

  • Increase the publicity of your business to help you grow to where you want to be.

  • Increase the number of potential clients through the promotion of your social media pages.
